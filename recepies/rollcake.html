  • Fruity Swiss Roll Cake

  • Soft, spongy cake rolled with a fruity filling—a perfect treat!

  • Fruity Swiss roll cake is a delightful dessert featuring a light, fluffy sponge cake rolled with a fresh, fruity filling. The combination of tender cake and vibrant fruit makes this a refreshing and indulgent treat. Perfect for afternoon tea, parties, or a sweet snack, this roll cake can be customized with different fruits to suit your taste.

  • Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs

  • 1 cup granulated sugar

  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour

  • 1 tsp baking powder

  • ¼ tsp salt

  • ¼ cup milk

  • 1 tbsp butter, melted

  • 1 cup mixed fresh fruits (e.g., strawberries, blueberries, kiwi, or mango)

  • ¾ cup heavy cream

  • 2 tbsp powdered sugar

  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

  • Instructions:

  • 1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a 10x15-inch baking sheet with parchment paper.

  • 2. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and sugar together until pale and fluffy, about 5 minutes. Add the vanilla extract and mix well.

  • 3. Sift together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually fold the dry ingredients into the egg mixture until just combined.

  • 4. Add the milk and melted butter, gently folding them in to ensure the batter is smooth and well-mixed.

  • 5. Pour the batter onto the prepared baking sheet and spread it out evenly.

  • 6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the cake is golden and springs back when lightly touched.

  • 7. While the cake bakes, prepare the filling. Whip the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract in a chilled bowl until soft peaks form.

  • 8. Once the cake is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Place a clean kitchen towel on a flat surface and dust it with powdered sugar. Carefully flip the cake onto the towel, peel off the parchment paper, and roll the cake up in the towel. Let it cool completely in this rolled-up shape.

  • 9. Once the cake has cooled, unroll it and spread a layer of whipped cream over the surface. Arrange the fresh fruit on top of the cream.

  • 10. Carefully roll the cake back up, making sure to keep the filling inside.

  • 11. Slice and serve the fruity Swiss roll cake! Optionally, dust with powdered sugar for an extra touch.

  • Pro Tip: Experiment with different fruits for various flavor combinations. You can even add a drizzle of chocolate for extra indulgence!

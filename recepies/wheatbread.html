  • Classic Whole Wheat Bread (Dairy-Free)

  • Hearty, wholesome, and perfect for sandwiches or toast!

  • This classic whole wheat bread is soft on the inside with a perfectly golden crust. Made with simple ingredients and no dairy, it’s a nutritious and delicious choice for any meal. Whether used for sandwiches, toast, or enjoyed on its own, this homemade bread is a must-try for bread lovers.

  • Ingredients:

  • 3 cups whole wheat flour

  • 1 ½ cups warm water (about 110°F/45°C)

  • 2 tbsp olive oil or vegetable oil

  • 2 tbsp maple syrup or honey (if not strictly vegan)

  • 2 ¼ tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast

  • 1 ½ tsp salt

  • Instructions:

  • 1. In a small bowl, mix warm water, yeast, and maple syrup (or honey). Let sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.

  • 2. In a large mixing bowl, combine whole wheat flour and salt.

  • 3. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour. Stir until a dough forms.

  • 4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.

  • 5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.

  • 6. Punch down the dough, shape it into a loaf, and place it into a greased loaf pan.

  • 7. Cover and let rise for another 30 minutes.

  • 8. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the top is golden brown.

  • 9. Remove from the oven and let cool before slicing.

  • Pro Tip: Brush the top with a little olive oil for a softer crust!

    • Classic Apple Pie

    • A timeless dessert with a flaky crust and a warm, spiced apple filling!

    • Classic apple pie is a beloved dessert featuring a buttery, flaky crust filled with tender, spiced apples. The perfect balance of sweetness and warmth makes it an all-time favorite, ideal for holidays, family gatherings, or simply satisfying a sweet craving. Serve it warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for the ultimate treat!

    • Ingredients:

    • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour

    • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, cold and cubed

    • ½ tsp salt

    • 1 tbsp granulated sugar

    • 6-8 tbsp ice water

    • 6 cups apples, peeled, cored, and sliced

    • ¾ cup granulated sugar

    • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour

    • 1 tsp cinnamon

    • ¼ tsp nutmeg

    • 1 tbsp lemon juice

    • 1 egg (for egg wash)

    • Instructions:

    • 1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9-inch pie dish.

    • 2. In a large bowl, mix flour, salt, and sugar. Cut in the cold butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

    • 3. Gradually add ice water, stirring until the dough comes together. Divide into two disks, wrap in plastic, and refrigerate for 30 minutes.

    • 4. In another bowl, combine apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Toss to coat.

    • 5. Roll out one dough disk and fit it into the pie dish. Add the apple filling.

    • 6. Roll out the second dough disk and place it over the filling. Seal and crimp the edges, then cut small slits for ventilation.

    • 7. Brush the crust with a beaten egg for a golden finish.

    • 8. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the filling is bubbling.

    • 9. Let cool before slicing. Serve warm with ice cream or whipped cream.

    • Pro Tip: Use a mix of tart and sweet apples for a well-balanced flavor!

    • Enjoy your homemade classic apple pie!

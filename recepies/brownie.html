  • Fudgy Brownies

  • These brownies are rich, dense, and super fudgy-everything a brownie should be!

  • Fudgy brownies are rich, dense, and indulgently moist treats that have a soft, gooey center and a slightly crisp top. Unlike cake-like brownies, which are lighter and fluffier, fudgy brownies have a compact, almost fudgy texture, thanks to the high ratio of butter and sugar to flour. The perfect fudgy brownie should melt in your mouth, delivering a deep, intense chocolate flavor with a rich, velvety finish. When cut, they should hold their shape but feel moist and almost sticky in the center, with small cracks forming on the top during baking. They're the ultimate chocolate lover’s dream – decadent, chewy, and irresistibly delicious!

  • Ingridients:

  • ½ cup (1 stick) unsalted butter

  • ½ cup semi-sweet chocolate, chopped

  • ¾ cup granulated sugar

  • ¼ cup brown sugar

  • 2 eggs

  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

  • ½ cup all-purpose flour

  • ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder

  • ¼ tsp salt

  • Instructions:

  • 1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line an 8x8-inch baking pan with parchment paper.

  • 2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter and chocolate together in 20-second intervals, stirring in between, until smooth.

  • 3. Whisk in the granulated sugar and brown sugar while the mixture is still warm.

  • 4. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, whisking until fully combined and glossy.

  • 5. Sift in the flour, cocoa powder, and salt, then gently fold until just combined (don't overmix).

  • 6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 22-25 minutes (until the center is set but still slightly gooey).

  • 7. Let cool for at least 30 minutes before slicing for the best fudgy texture.

  • Pro Tip: For extra gooey brownies, underbake slightly and chill before cutting. You can also mix in chocolate chips or a pinch of espresso powder for a deeper flavor!

  • Enjoy your rich, fudgy brownies!

  • Cheesy Garlic Bread

  • Golden, crispy, and loaded with gooey cheese—this garlic bread is the ultimate side dish!

  • Cheesy garlic bread is the perfect combination of crispy, buttery bread infused with garlic and melted cheese. This indulgent side dish features a golden, crispy crust with a soft, fluffy interior, making it a must-have for pasta nights, soups, or as a standalone treat. Made with fresh garlic, herbs, and plenty of cheese, each bite is packed with rich, savory flavors that will leave you craving more!

  • Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf of French or Italian bread

  • ½ cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped

  • ½ tsp salt

  • ½ tsp black pepper

  • ½ tsp Italian seasoning (optional)

  • Instructions:

  • 1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  • 2. Slice the bread in half lengthwise and place it on the prepared baking sheet.

  • 3. In a bowl, mix melted butter, minced garlic, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.

  • 4. Spread the garlic butter mixture evenly over both halves of the bread.

  • 5. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheese generously over the top.

  • 6. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  • 7. Turn on the broiler for 1-2 minutes for an extra crispy, golden top.

  • 8. Remove from the oven, sprinkle with fresh parsley, and let cool slightly before slicing.

  • Pro Tip: Add a drizzle of olive oil or a pinch of red pepper flakes for extra flavor!

  • Enjoy your delicious, cheesy garlic bread!

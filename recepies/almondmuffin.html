  • Almond Flour Muffins (Gluten-Free, Dairy-Free)

  • Light, fluffy, and naturally gluten-free—these almond flour muffins are a healthy and delicious treat!

  • These almond flour muffins are the perfect gluten-free and dairy-free alternative to traditional muffins. They have a tender crumb, a naturally sweet nutty flavor, and are packed with wholesome ingredients. Perfect for breakfast, snacks, or a guilt-free dessert, these muffins are moist, flavorful, and easy to make!

  • Ingredients:

  • 2 cups almond flour

  • ½ tsp baking soda

  • ¼ tsp salt

  • 3 eggs

  • ¼ cup maple syrup or honey

  • ¼ cup coconut oil, melted

  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

  • ½ cup dairy-free milk (such as almond or coconut milk)

  • ½ cup add-ins (e.g., chocolate chips, blueberries, or chopped nuts - optional)

  • Instructions:

  • 1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a muffin tin with paper liners or lightly grease it.

  • 2. In a large bowl, whisk together the almond flour, baking soda, and salt.

  • 3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, maple syrup (or honey), melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, and dairy-free milk.

  • 4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined.

  • 5. Fold in any add-ins, if using.

  • 6. Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups, filling each about ¾ full.

  • 7. Bake for 18-22 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  • 8. Allow to cool in the pan for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

  • Pro Tip: Add a sprinkle of cinnamon or a handful of chopped nuts for extra flavor!

  • Enjoy your delicious, gluten-free almond flour muffins!

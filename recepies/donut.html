  • Homemade Donuts

  • Soft, fluffy, and freshly fried—these homemade donuts are a delightful treat!

  • These homemade donuts are the perfect sweet indulgence. Soft, airy, and fried to golden perfection, they are a family favorite. Whether you enjoy them plain, glazed, or covered with powdered sugar, these donuts are sure to satisfy your cravings and impress your guests. Perfect for breakfast or a dessert snack!

  • Ingredients:

  • 2 ¼ tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast

  • ¾ cup warm milk (about 110°F/45°C)

  • ¼ cup granulated sugar

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour

  • ½ tsp salt

  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon

  • 2 large eggs

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened

  • ¼ cup water

  • Vegetable oil, for frying

  • Glaze: 1 cup powdered sugar, 2 tbsp milk, 1 tsp vanilla extract

  • Optional toppings: sprinkles, chocolate glaze, powdered sugar

  • Instructions:

  • 1. In a small bowl, mix the warm milk, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes, until the yeast becomes foamy.

  • 2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, and cinnamon. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture, eggs, and softened butter. Stir until a dough begins to form.

  • 3. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.

  • 4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

  • 5. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out to about ½ inch thickness. Use a donut cutter or two round cutters (one large, one small) to cut out the donut shapes.

  • 6. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pot or frying pan to 350°F (175°C). Carefully fry the donuts in batches, cooking for about 1-2 minutes per side, until golden brown.

  • 7. Remove the donuts from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.

  • 8. To make the glaze, whisk together powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.

  • 9. Dip the warm donuts into the glaze, allowing excess to drip off, and place them on a wire rack to set.

  • 10. Optional: top with sprinkles, chocolate glaze, or powdered sugar for extra flavor and decoration.

  • Pro Tip: For a delicious twist, add a sprinkle of cinnamon sugar while the donuts are still warm!

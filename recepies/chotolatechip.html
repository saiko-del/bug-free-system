  • Nut-Free Chocolate Chips

  • Delicious, allergy-friendly chocolate chips—perfect for baking and snacking!

  • Nut-free chocolate chips are a great option for those with nut allergies or sensitivities. These homemade chocolate chips are rich, smooth, and melt perfectly in cookies, brownies, or any baked goods. Made with just a few simple ingredients, they deliver a deep chocolate flavor without any risk of cross-contamination, making them a safe and delicious treat for everyone!

  • Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cocoa butter

  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder

  • ½ cup powdered sugar

  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

  • ⅛ tsp salt

  • Instructions:

  • 1. Melt the cocoa butter in a double boiler over low heat, stirring occasionally.

  • 2. Once fully melted, whisk in the cocoa powder, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until smooth.

  • 3. Pour the mixture into a piping bag or use a small spoon to drop chocolate chips onto a parchment-lined baking sheet.

  • 4. Let the chocolate chips set at room temperature or refrigerate for quicker results.

  • 5. Once hardened, store in an airtight container at room temperature or in the fridge for longer freshness.

  • Pro Tip: Use silicone molds for uniform chocolate chip shapes!

  • Enjoy your homemade, nut-free chocolate chips!

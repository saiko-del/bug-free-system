  • Classic New York Cheesecake

  • New York Cheesecake is rich, dense, and creamy with a smooth texture and a buttery graham cracker crust.

  • Cheesecake is a timeless dessert that balances the tanginess of cream cheese with the sweetness of sugar and vanilla. A well-made cheesecake should be smooth, luscious, and just the right amount of firm. Whether topped with fresh berries, caramel, or chocolate, it's always a crowd-pleaser!

  • Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (200g) graham cracker crumbs

  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter, melted

  • 2 (8oz) packages cream cheese, softened

  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar

  • 3 large eggs

  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

  • 1 cup (240ml) sour cream

  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

  • Instructions:

  • 1. Preheat oven to 325°F (163°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.

  • 2. Mix graham cracker crumbs with melted butter and press into the bottom of the pan.

  • 3. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth.

  • 4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing after each addition.

  • 5. Mix in vanilla extract, sour cream, and lemon juice until well combined.

  • 6. Pour filling over the crust and smooth the top.

  • 7. Bake for 50-60 minutes until the edges are set but the center still jiggles slightly.

  • 8. Turn off the oven and let the cheesecake cool inside with the door slightly open.

  • 9. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours (preferably overnight) before serving.

  • 10. Top with fresh berries, whipped cream, or caramel before serving.

  • Classic French Baguette

  • A crispy crust with a soft, airy interior—this French baguette is the perfect homemade bread!

  • The classic French baguette is a staple of French baking, known for its golden, crispy crust and chewy, airy crumb. Made with just a few simple ingredients, this traditional bread requires patience and technique to achieve its signature texture. Perfect for sandwiches, dipping, or enjoying with butter, this homemade baguette brings the taste of France right to your kitchen!

  • Ingredients:

  • 3 ½ cups (440g) bread flour

  • 1 ½ tsp salt

  • 1 tsp active dry yeast

  • 1 ¼ cups (300ml) warm water

  • Instructions:

  • 1. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.

  • 2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Gradually add the yeast mixture, stirring until a dough forms.

  • 3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10 minutes, until smooth and elastic.

  • 4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let rise for 1 ½ to 2 hours, or until doubled in size.

  • 5. Punch down the dough and divide it into two equal portions. Shape each into a baguette by rolling into a long, narrow log.

  • 6. Place the shaped dough onto a parchment-lined baking sheet, cover lightly, and let rise for another 30-45 minutes.

  • 7. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C). Place a pan of hot water on the bottom rack to create steam.

  • 8. Score the tops of the baguettes with a sharp knife and bake for 20-25 minutes, until golden brown.

  • 9. Let cool on a wire rack before slicing.

  • Pro Tip: For an extra crispy crust, spray the oven with water right before baking!

  • Enjoy your freshly baked French baguette!

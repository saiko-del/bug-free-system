  • Vegan Banana Bread (Egg & Dairy-Free)

  • Moist, flavorful, and naturally sweet—this banana bread is perfect for any occasion!

  • This vegan banana bread is soft, moist, and packed with banana flavor, all without eggs or dairy! Made with simple plant-based ingredients, it's a delicious and wholesome treat for breakfast, snacks, or dessert. Serve it warm with a spread of vegan butter or nut butter for extra indulgence.

  • Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed

  • ⅓ cup coconut oil or vegetable oil

  • ½ cup maple syrup or brown sugar

  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour

  • 1 tsp baking soda

  • ½ tsp salt

  • ½ tsp cinnamon

  • ½ cup non-dairy milk (almond, soy, or oat)

  • ½ cup chopped walnuts or chocolate chips (optional)

  • Instructions:

  • 1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a loaf pan or line it with parchment paper.

  • 2. In a large bowl, whisk together the mashed bananas, oil, maple syrup (or sugar), and vanilla extract.

  • 3. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.

  • 4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined.

  • 5. Stir in the non-dairy milk and fold in walnuts or chocolate chips if using.

  • 6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top.

  • 7. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

  • 8. Let the banana bread cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack.

  • 9. Slice and enjoy your delicious vegan banana bread!

  • Pro Tip: Add a sprinkle of oats or extra banana slices on top before baking for a decorative touch!
